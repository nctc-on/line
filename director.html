Director's Message

A Vision for Excellence in Education

Sahid Aktar

Director, NCTC

Education is not just about learning; it's about transforming lives and building futures through technology.

Dear Students and Parents,

Welcome to National Computer Training Centre (NCTC). As the Director of this esteemed institution, it gives me immense pleasure to share our vision and commitment to excellence in computer education.

At NCTC, we believe in providing not just education, but a complete learning experience that prepares our students for the digital age. Our focus is on practical knowledge, industry-relevant skills, and character building.

We have created an environment where learning is continuous, innovation is encouraged, and excellence is pursued. Our modern facilities, experienced faculty, and industry-aligned curriculum ensure that our students receive the best possible technical education.

I invite you to be part of our journey towards digital excellence and success.

Sahid Aktar

Director, NCTC
