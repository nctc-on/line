Admission Process

Follow these steps to complete your offline admission

1

Visit Our Campus

Visit our campus during office hours (Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 5 PM)

2

Collect Admission Form

Obtain the admission form from our admission office

3

Submit Documents

Submit the filled form along with required documents

4

Complete Payment

Pay the admission fees at our accounts department

Required Documents

Identity Proof

Aadhar Card / Voter ID / Passport

Educational Documents

Previous education certificates and marksheets

Photographs

4 recent passport size photographs

Address Proof

Recent utility bill / Rental agreement
