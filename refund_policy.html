Refund Policy

Important Notice

ELECTROMART maintains a strict no-refund policy for all course fees and related payments. This policy is in place to ensure the sustainability and quality of our educational services.

1. No Refund Policy

All payments made to NATIONAL COMPUTER TRAINING CENTRE, including but not limited to:

  • Course registration fees
  • Tuition fees
  • Examination fees
  • Certification fees

are non-refundable under any circumstances.

2. Commitment to Quality

We are committed to providing high-quality education and training. Our no-refund policy helps us maintain this commitment by ensuring proper resource allocation and course planning.

3. Course Completion

Students are encouraged to complete their enrolled courses. We provide comprehensive support and resources to help students succeed in their chosen programs.
